Frequently Asked Questions

Some frequently asked questions about my policies for commissions.

Do you need to pay upfront in full?

Yes, because you are paying for the usage of my materials and for my time.

Can I cancel my commission half way through?

No, it's not common place to do so and your payment is not going to be refunded because half of your drawing will already be done.

Can I cancel my commission in the beginning processes?

Yes, as long as I am given ample time to cancel your request.

Is there anything you can't draw?

I cannot draw anthros or furries currently at the moment.
